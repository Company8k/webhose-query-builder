country(c) → {this}

Webhose.io uses heuristics to determine the country origin of a site, by taking into account the site's IP, TLD and language. Many times the country origin isn't conclusive so it isn't set, therefor filtering by country may result in much less data than when filtering by language. Return posts from sites from Hong Kong: thread.country:HK To get the full country code list, visit countrycode.org.
